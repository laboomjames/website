why build in public?

building in public makes you think clearer. like, when you know someone else might read about your process, you naturally get better at explaining your reasoning and documenting decisions
it's less about perfection and more about making your thoughts make sense—both for yourself and others
it's also like a personal progress tracker. looking back on old projects? instant reminder of how far you've come. and, bonus, your journey might help someone else who's just starting out

my first two projects

i kicked things off with two simple apps that solved problems i'd actually run into:

a wim hof breathing exercise app w cyberpunk theme

a minimal pomodoro timer for focus

both incredibly simple, but they help me practice the fundamentals for tools i could use right away

looking forward?

i'm hyped to take on more complex projects and keep sharing along the way. building in public isn't just showing off—it's starting conversations with people on similar paths

and honestly, i can't wait to see where it all leads :)
